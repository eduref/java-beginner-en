Why programming?

  • The IT sector is a very important industry.
  • 60 % of all start-ups are active in the IT sector.
  • More than 50% of all products build directly on IT.
  • The share of IT in German economic output is higher than the share of the automotive industry.
  • A basic understanding of IT is necessary for most career starters.
